Anxiety Helper

Welcome to Anxiety Helper

Anxiety Helper is an app designed to help aid students handle their anxiety by providing different solutions to fit with the different triggers and levels of the anxiety. You can hop onto My Page and input what triggers or triggered the anxiety and you will be given different solutions that should help you get through it. Remember each person is different, so if the suggested solutions do not work, look through the list and see what does help.
